Familiar:
User-based Design: UX should always revolve around the user's needs and goals. The importance of understanding user behavior and motivations before diving into design solutions was a familiar concept.
Usability Principles: A good design should consist of simplicity, consistency, and feedback. Test with users.
Learned:
Stability Principles: Despite technological advancements, the fundamental principles of UX design have remained the same for decades. This gave me a perspective on the long-term value of learning.
UX Design Process: UX design processes are systematic approaches to creating meaningful and relevant experiences for users. They usually involve research, ideation, prototyping, testing and implementation.
